Help

Database Fields

  • ID: Database entry ID.
  • DateTime: Date and time of the measurement.
  • TempIn: Temperature inside the device (°C).
  • TempOut: Outside temperature (°C).
  • TempInOffset, TempOutOffset: Used temperature offsets (°C).
  • Weight: Ice weight (g).
  • WeightRaw: Raw device dependant weight value eg. voltage.
  • Status: Device status.
    • 0: MEASURE
    • 1: DEICE
  • Errors: Bitwise OR'd list of errors.
    • 1: TempIn measurement failed.
    • 2: TempOut measurement failed.
    • 4: Weight measurement failed.
